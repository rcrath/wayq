# WayQ v0.1.1-alpha

First public alpha of WayQ — a fast, deceptively simple two-mode filter, light on CPU and quick to dial in. VST3 and CLAP on Windows and Linux; VST3, CLAP, and AU on macOS.

**Pass** — a standard HP / Bell / LP first-pass EQ. One handle each sweeps the high-pass and low-pass cutoff and steepness, with a freq/gain/Q bell for shaping in between.

**Baxandall** — two tilt filters, bass and treble, that see-saw against each other, plus a bell. Raise the treble and the bass eases back, and vice versa — a phantom midrange you can't get from the pass filters. Low and low makes a mid bump; high and high makes a smile; and the yin and yang handles set where the tilts pivot — close together for a narrow mid, far apart for a broad shelf.

This is an early alpha — expect rough edges. Free software under the GNU GPL v3.
